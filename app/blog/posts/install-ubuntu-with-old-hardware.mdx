## Backgrounds
I have a bunch of old PC hardwares in the cloest, so i figure why not build a ubunutu rig cuz i absolutely want to separte my main gaming rig from the other tasks. Ubunutu being a free and lightweight system with great compatibility for my needs is just what i need.

## PC Hardware Lists
- **CPU**: Ryzen 3700X
- **Motherboard**: ASUS X570 TUF WiFi
- **GPU**: **NVIDIA GTX 750 Ti ASUS 2FAN** \<- This is the exact model in the game *Esacpe from Tarkov*
- **RAM**: Trident Z DDR4 3200mHz 8G x 4
